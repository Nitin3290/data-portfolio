Customer Churn Prediction

Behavior Analysis with Logistic Regression & Decision Trees

🧠 Project Summary

This project focuses on identifying customers who are most likely to churn in an e-commerce platform based on behavioral and transactional data. The aim was to proactively detect patterns that signal potential exits and help businesses take preventive action.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

Python, Jupyter Notebook, Scikit-learn, Pandas, Matplotlib

📌 Approach

  • Performed data cleaning and feature engineering to handle nulls, encode categoricals, and scale numeric fields.
  • Implemented both Logistic Regression and Decision Tree classifiers.
  • Evaluated models using accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score.
  • Visualized feature importance and customer segments with highest churn likelihood.

🎯 Outcome

The final model achieved an 87% precision in flagging at-risk users. Business teams can now target these users with loyalty offers and interventions, improving retention and long-term customer value.
